Since October of 2024, Alfredo has worked at the Patterson dining hall.

in teams, maintain a clean workspace, and provide exceptional customer service to
the students. In May of 2025, Alfredo was promoted to Student Manager, where he
currently works with students to guide them and help them learn their responsibilities
at work.

Bookstore Associate

Before working at Patterson, Alfredo worked at the Follett bookstore
on campus as a bookstore associate. There, he was able to help students find their
textbooks, assist them with any questions they had, and help them check out their
items. Alfredo learned how to work in a fast-paced environment and how to provide
excellent customer service. He also learned how to work with a team and how to
communicate effectively with his coworkers.
